Priority Rules

1
RED overrides everything

Dangerous, reported, or very low rating (<2.5) always shows RED, even with recent reviews.

2
Recent review = GREEN

Any review within 6 months makes spot GREEN, regardless of age or rating (unless RED).

3
Good rating = GREEN

Rating ≥ 3.5 makes spot GREEN, even if old (unless RED).

4
Age-based degradation

18+ months without review = ORANGE. 6-18 months = YELLOW.
